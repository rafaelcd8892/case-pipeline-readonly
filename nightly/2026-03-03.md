# 2026-03-03

## Attorney Daily Appointments Page (Phase 3)

Attorneys can now go to `/appointments` and see all their appointments for today (or the full week) in one place, with full client context right on the card. No more flipping between Monday.com boards to prepare for meetings.

### What it does

- **Attorney filter** — pick a specific attorney (R, M, LB, WH) or view everyone's schedule at once. Your selection is remembered between visits.
- **Today / This Week** — toggle to see just today or the whole week ahead.
- **Three detail levels** — switch between Minimal (just names), Snapshot (case counts, deadlines, contact info), and Full (complete case breakdown with contracts and board items).
- **Notes timeline** — expand any appointment card to see the client's recent notes and updates, so you walk into the meeting with context. This reuses the same timeline from the 360 client view.
- **Quick link to 360** — every card links directly to the client's full profile.

### Why it matters

Attorneys used to prep for meetings by manually searching each client. Now they open one page, see who's coming in, what cases are active, and what the latest notes say. Less prep time, better meetings.

### What's next

This is the first page that will benefit from editing — updating appointment status, adding pre-meeting notes, rescheduling. That write-back feature is documented and marked in the code for future implementation.

## Enhanced Search & Filters (Phase 4)

Staff can now find clients by phone number, email, or street address — not just name. The search bar has a type dropdown to search across contracts, court cases, open forms, and other case types from one place.

### What it does

- **Smart search** — type the last 4 digits of a phone number and the matching client pops up. Search by partial email or street address too.
- **Search type dropdown** — switch between Clients, Contracts, Court Cases, Open Forms, Motions, Appeals, FOIAs, RFEs, Litigation, and I-918B.
- **Filtered client list** — the `/clients` page has priority buttons, status/attorney/board type dropdowns, and a date range picker. Mix and match any combination.
- **Shareable filter URLs** — filters sync to the URL, so you can bookmark or send a filtered view to a coworker. The app also remembers your last-used priority and attorney.
- **Clickable KPI cards** — the count numbers on the dashboard are now clickable. Tap a number to see all matching clients, not just the top 5 preview.

### Why it matters

Finding a client used to require knowing their exact name. Now a quick phone number or address lookup works. The filter bar lets staff quickly narrow down to "high-priority clients with court cases this month" or "all clients with pending contracts." Clickable KPI counts close the gap between the dashboard overview and actionable data.
