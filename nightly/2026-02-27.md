# 2026-02-27

## Landing Page Dashboard (Phase 2)

The app now opens to a manager dashboard instead of a blank "Go to Clients" splash. The landing page shows five KPI cards that summarize the firm's current workload at a glance, so staff can prioritize without drilling into individual clients first.

### What it shows

- **Open Forms** — how many client forms are currently in progress
- **Pending Contracts** — Fee Ks waiting for review, payment, or action
- **Paid Fee Ks** — contracts that are paid and need someone to open the case
- **Upcoming Deadlines** — anything due in the next 7 days across all boards
- **Upcoming Hearings** — court hearings coming up, with a toggle between "7 days" and "this month"

Each card shows the total count and the top 5 items. Every item links to the client's 360 page so you can jump straight to context.

### Why it matters

Before this, the only way to find urgent work was to search client by client. Now the landing page surfaces what needs attention today — overdue deadlines, pending contracts, hearings on the horizon — without anyone having to dig.
